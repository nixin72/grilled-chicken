The Goldendoodle is a cross-breed dog, which is obtained by breeding a Golden Retriever with a Poodle. The name, which alters "poodle" to "doodle" by analogy to "Labradoodle", was coined in 1992. Wikipedia.

  • Temperament: Energetic, Friendly, Trainable, Intelligent.
  • Height: 58-63 cm
  • Weight: 15 - 30pounds
  • Life span: 12 – 15 years

The Goldendoodle is an affectionate and gentle dog that has gained popularity since they were first developed in 1990s. Still a young when compared to other designer breeds, and many of today's litters are the results of first-generation breedings between Poodles and Golden Retrievers.
