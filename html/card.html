Basset Hound

French aristocrats bred these dogs from bloodhounds to use while hunting

  • Temperament: Tenacious, Devoted, Gentle, Friendly.
  • Height: 30-38 inches
  • Weight: 51 to 64 pounds
  • Life span: 10 – 12 years

Bassets are scent hounds, meaning that they were bred to hunt by following the scent of their prey. ... The Basset Hound has a rounded skull with a deep muzzle and a lot of loose skin on the face, which is heavily wrinkled over the brow when the dog is tracking.
